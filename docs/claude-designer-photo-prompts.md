## Tips for whichever AI image tool you use

- **Run each prompt 3–4 times** and pick the best result — AI portrait consistency varies per generation.
- **Tim's face should match across all shots.** If the tool drifts, paste a reference URL or upload one of the existing two portraits as a style anchor.
- **Hands and tools** are where AI image gen most often glitches. Re-roll if you see anatomy weirdness.
- **Resolution:** generate at 1024×1280 or higher. Downsize for web via `sharp` to 1000×1250 JPEG quality 82.
- **Save the AI-generated images** under `docs/photography-staging/` and have me wire them in — or drop them straight into `apps/web/public/photography/` with the suggested filenames.
